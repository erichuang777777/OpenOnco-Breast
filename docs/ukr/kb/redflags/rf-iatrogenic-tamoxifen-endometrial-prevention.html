Походження тривожної ознаки

ВизначенняCurrent or prior tamoxifen exposure ≥5 years in a woman with intact uterus (no prior hysterectomy). Tamoxifen is a selective estrogen- receptor modulator (SERM) — antagonist in breast tissue (therapeutic for ER+ breast cancer + risk reduction) but partial agonist in endometrium → ~2-7 fold increased endometrial cancer risk (predominantly endometrioid; less commonly serous, carcinosarcoma). Cumulative risk ~1-2% over 5y treatment; higher with longer duration + post-menopausal status. Endometrial cancers in tamoxifen-exposed women often more aggressive histology than spontaneous. Population with hysterectomy: this RF does NOT fire (no at-risk organ). Patient with ER+ breast cancer (treatment-time persona) ALSO has prevention- persona considerations covered by this RF. Prevention-persona RedFlag (§20 v0.3 iatrogenic pilot).

Нотатки

Prevention-persona RedFlag for tamoxifen exposure ≥5y in women with intact uterus. ~2-7 fold endometrial cancer risk. Engine routes to PreventionPlan recommending symptom-prompted evaluation (postmenopausal bleeding → immediate workup with TVUS + endometrial biopsy). Routine asymptomatic TVUS screening NOT recommended per ACOG (false-positives from tamoxifen-related subepithelial changes). STUB pending two-Clinical-Co-Lead signoff per CHARTER §6.1 dev-mode.
