What NOT to do

Explicit prohibitive rules, each grounded in a regimen / supportive care / contraindication entity
Standard plan (IND-CHRONIC-HIV-PREVENTION-ART)
  • Do not skip drug-drug interaction screening before any cancer treatment in an HIV+ patient.
  • Do not discontinue ART during cancer treatment without an HIV-specialist consult.
  • Do not use efavirenz-based regimens in patients with a history of depression / suicidality.
Active surveillance (watch-and-wait) (IND-CHRONIC-HIV-PREVENTION-OBSERVATION)
  • Do not present observation as equivalent to ART — ART is a life-saving intervention, not merely cancer prevention.
  • Do not skip periodic re-counseling about the importance of ART — most patients eventually accept therapy after additional visits.
  • Do not use this pathway as a first choice — the ART track remains the preferred standard of care.
